Stages of building LLMs

Creating an LLM = Pretraining + Fine tuning
Pretraining -training on Large, diverse amount of dataset. - gets answers
Finetuning - Refinement by training on narrower dataset, specific to a particular task or domain. - this is exact and based on your requirement high accurate

Example of Finetuning - Chatgpt can know all but specific on any large area that's due to the Pretraining.
Harvey.ai - a ai for law. Finetuning done based on Law and legal cases.

Finetuning is based on their own data to make thier product much stronger in ai , in that category. its needs for a product to work on a specific category ai.


Large training data sources - Common crawl + WebText2 + Books1 + Books2 + Wikipedia - From each source, 3 billions of tokens are taken to train models.

NLP - if we use it we need to train each NLP for different usecase like a NLP for quiz, NLP for chatbot, NLP of lang translation.
But an LLM can easily fulfill all at a time.


Pretraining + Finetuning schema:

Data- Internet text, books, media, research articles(raw unlabelled text - trillions of words) --->  
pretrained LLM(foundational model -> LLM pretrained on unlabelled text data) ---> 
pretrained LLM trained on labelled dataset - finetuned LLM (classifiaction, summarisation, translation, personal assistants)- Finetune based on own data(labelled dataset)

Steps for building LLM:
1. train on a large corpus of text(raw text = without any labels ) from Internet.
2. First training stage of LLM is also called foundational model
3. After obtaining the pretrained LLM , we can further train LLM on a labelled data. -> Finetuning
4. There are two popular categories of Finetuning.
- instruction Finetuning - labelled dataset consists of instruction- answer pairs.
ex: text translation, airline customer support

- finetuning for classifiaction tasks - labelled dataset consists of text text and associated labels
eg: emails spam vs no-spam
