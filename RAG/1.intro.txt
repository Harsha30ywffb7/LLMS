How computers understand the languages while doing these NLP and all?

To help computers to understand we need:
- Text preprocessing
- Tokenisation
- Embeddings

Step-1: Text preprocessing

Prepare Text by:
- Converting all into lowercases
- Removing punctuations
- Remove stop words
- Stemming/Lemmatisation -- gets root forms
--- change, changing, changes, changer, changed ==== chang (Stemming)
--- change, changing, changes, changer, changed ==== change (Lemmatisation) - more meaningful


Step-2: Tokenisation

- Break words into smaller parts

-- Word Tokenisation
-- subword Tokenisation

Step-3: Embeddings
- Represent the words as numbers computers can understand similarities or find relationships between those words
-- Turns words into vectors
-- similar words(king, queen have similar vectors)
- Already there are so many word to vector mapping algorithms are there one of them is Word2vec.
- eg: king queen , man woman in this way.
