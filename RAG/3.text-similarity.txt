It is how much the text related to each other.

It helps computers to similarity by the assigning the scores to the texts by mathematical scoring.
If user asks a question this similarity scores help to find more closer words.

High similarity scores mean more relevant content, so it ranked in higher for the chatbot responses.


Cosine similarity:
it shows the how similar two pieces of the text are by comparing the direction of thier meaning rather than the exact words.
it works by measuring angle between the two vectors of the words.

Jaccard similarity:
focuses on comparing words directly, it measures the similarity between two sets of words how many are common.

Euclidean distance:
Calculates the straight line difference between two vectors. smaller the mean texts are more similar.
