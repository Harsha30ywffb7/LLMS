attention mechanism

what is attention?
- it assigns a weights to each word to show how important it is in the context.
- attention helps the model to zoom in the words which matter most for the task.

example: if we have 100k words, then it needs to focus on the fewer words based on weightage. to decrease the computational expensives.

How does attention works?

step-1: Assign importance to words
- Each word in a sentence gets an importance score based on the meaning and other relation of words.

ex: the cat which was sleeping, jumped over the wall.
(cat, jumped)- importance

step-2: calculate the attention scores
- words relatively closer have higher scores. scores are calculated based on the relationships. relative words get higher scores.

ex: cat and jumped gets higher scores because they are directly related.

step-3: adjust focus based on the scores.
- the model uses these scores to focus on the higher scoring words, making sense of the main meaning of the sentence.

ex: cat, jumped, wall help to understand the action.

--This is how the chatgpt understands our context and focus on the words by adjusting scores.This is how the attention works.
